Frequently Asked Questions

How can I submit a news tip?

You can submit news tips through our contact form, email us directly at editorial@eastafricanpost.com, or call our hotline at +254 700 000 999 for urgent matters.

How do I advertise with you?

For advertising inquiries, please contact our advertising team at advertising@eastafricanpost.com or call +254 700 000 002.

Can I contribute articles?

We welcome contributions from freelance writers and experts. Please send your pitch to editorial@eastafricanpost.com with a brief outline of your proposed article.

How do I report an error?

If you spot an error in our content, please email us at editorial@eastafricanpost.com with the specific details and we'll correct it promptly.

About The East African Post

Your premier source for comprehensive news coverage from across East Africa

Our Mission

The East African Post is dedicated to bringing you the most accurate, timely, and comprehensive news coverage from across the East African region. We believe in the power of informed citizens and strive to provide balanced, objective reporting that serves the diverse communities of Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, and Burundi.

Our mission is to be the most trusted source of news and information for East Africans, both within the region and in the diaspora. We are committed to journalistic excellence, ethical reporting, and serving the public interest.

Our Values

Accuracy

We are committed to factual, verified reporting and correcting any errors promptly.

Independence

We maintain editorial independence and are not influenced by political or commercial interests.

Diversity

We celebrate the rich diversity of East Africa and ensure all voices are represented.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of journalistic ethics and professional conduct.

Our History

Founded in 2020, The East African Post emerged from a vision to create a comprehensive news platform that would serve the diverse communities of East Africa. What started as a small team of dedicated journalists has grown into one of the region's most trusted news sources.

Over the years, we have expanded our coverage, improved our digital presence, and built strong relationships with communities across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, and Burundi. Our commitment to quality journalism and regional cooperation has remained constant throughout our growth.
